Privacy Policy — Calc 3D

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Summary: Calc 3D is a cost calculation app for 3D printing. We collect the minimum amount of personal data possible, do not use trackers, do not display ads, and do not share your information with third parties. All your data is stored locally on your device or, if you choose, on your own Google Drive.

1. Who we are

Calc 3D ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by Axiom 3D Studio.

For questions related to this policy or your data, contact:

  • Email: axiomcalculator3d@gmail.com
  • Instagram: @axiom_3d_studio

2. Data we collect

2.1 Data you provide directly to the app

When using Calc 3D, you enter information about your 3D printing business, such as:

  • Project, part, and client names
  • Material, energy, and printer depreciation costs
  • Sales records, expenses, delivery agenda
  • Settings preferences (language, theme, currency, custom logo)

All this data is stored locally on your device, within the app's private storage (Android DataStore). We, the developers, do not have access to this data.

2.2 Data obtained via Google Sign-In (optional)

If you choose to sign in with your Google account (to enable Google Drive sync), the app receives the following information from Google:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name
  • Your public profile photo (URL)

This information is used solely to display in the Settings screen and identify the connected account for backup purposes. We do not store this data on external servers.

2.3 Data synced to your Google Drive (optional)

If you enable "Save to Drive", the app writes a backup file (calc3d_backup.json) to the app's private folder within your own Google Drive (appDataFolder). This folder:

  • Belongs exclusively to you
  • Is not visible in the common Google Drive interface (it's a hidden app folder)
  • Can be deleted at any time
  • The developer has no access to it — only you and your app

We use the drive.appdata OAuth scope, the most restrictive scope allowed by Google, which does not grant access to any other files in your Drive.

2.4 Data we do NOT collect

  • ❌ We do not collect location data (GPS)
  • ❌ We do not collect usage data for analytics
  • ❌ We do not access contacts, photos, microphone, or camera
  • ❌ We do not display third-party ads
  • ❌ We do not use advertising identifiers (AAID)
  • ❌ We do not sell your data

3. How we use data

The data you provide is used solely to:

  • Calculate costs and profit margins for your printed parts
  • Generate sales and expense reports
  • Enable backup and restoration of your own data
  • Customize the interface according to your preferences

4. Sharing with third parties

We do not share your data with third parties, except when strictly necessary for features you have opted to use:

  • Google LLC — when you use Sign-In or Drive, Google acts as the authentication and storage provider. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Storage and retention

  • Local data remains on your device while the app is installed. Uninstalling removes all local data.
  • Google Drive backups remain in your account until you delete them.
  • JSON file backups exported to the Downloads folder remain until you delete them.

6. Your rights (GDPR / LGPD)

You have the right to, at any time:

  • Access your data — all of it is visible within the app itself
  • Export your data — using the "Export Backup" button in Settings
  • Correct or delete your data — directly in the app, or via "Reset All"
  • Disconnect your Google account at any time, in Settings > Google Drive Sync
  • Revoke the app's access to your Google account at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

7. Security

Local data is stored in the app's private area, accessible only by Calc 3D on Android. Communications with Google Drive use OAuth 2.0 and HTTPS. We do not store passwords; authentication is managed entirely by Google.

8. Children

Calc 3D is intended for entrepreneurs and professionals aged 18 and older. The app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Android permissions used

  • INTERNET — required for Google Sign-In and Google Drive
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to detect when you are offline and disable cloud features
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 or lower only) — to save backups to the Downloads folder

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be modified when significant changes occur.
