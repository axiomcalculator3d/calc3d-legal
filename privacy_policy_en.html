Privacy Policy — Calc 3D

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Summary: Calc 3D is a cost calculation app for 3D printing businesses. We collect the minimum amount of personal data possible, we don't use trackers, we don't show ads, and we don't share your information with third parties. All your data stays on your device or, if you choose, in your own Google Drive.

1. Who we are

Calc 3D ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by Axiom 3D Studio.

For questions related to this policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: axiomcalculator3d@gmail.com
  • Instagram: @axiom_3d_studio

2. Data we collect

2.1 Data you provide directly to the app

When you use Calc 3D, you enter information about your 3D printing business, such as:

  • Project, part and client names
  • Material, energy and printer depreciation costs
  • Sales records, expenses, delivery schedule
  • Configuration preferences (language, theme, currency, custom logo)

All this data is stored locally on your device, inside the app's private storage area (Android DataStore). We, the developers, have no access to it.

2.2 Data obtained via Google Sign-In (optional)

If you choose to sign in with your Google account (to enable Google Drive sync), the app receives the following information from Google:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name
  • Your public profile picture (URL)

This information is used exclusively to show on the Settings screen and to identify the connected account for backup purposes. We do not store this data on any external server.

2.3 Data synced to your Google Drive (optional)

If you enable "Save to Drive", the app writes a backup file (calc3d_backup.json) to the app-private folder inside your own Google Drive (appDataFolder). This folder:

  • Belongs exclusively to you
  • Is not visible in the regular Google Drive interface (it is a hidden app folder)
  • Can be deleted at any time
  • Is not accessible to the developer — only to you and your app

We use the drive.appdata OAuth scope, which is the most restrictive scope Google allows and does not grant access to any other file in your Drive.

2.4 In-app purchases (optional)

Calc 3D offers a Pro plan through Google Play Billing, available in two options:

  • Pro Lifetime — one-time payment
  • Pro Monthly — recurring subscription, cancelable at any time

When you make a purchase, the payment process is handled entirely by Google Play. The app has no access to your card details, bank account or any other financial information. We only receive a validation token and the type of plan purchased, both stored locally on your device to unlock the Pro features. To manage or cancel subscriptions, use the Google Play app → Payments and subscriptions.

2.5 Sharing via WhatsApp and other external apps

When you tap "Send Quote", "Add to Google Calendar" or "Open in Bitcoin wallet", the app simply opens the other app with the content pre-filled — we don't send anything on your behalf. You review the content and decide whether to send/use it. Data leaving Calc 3D at that moment is governed by the privacy policy of the destination app (WhatsApp, Google Calendar, Bitcoin wallet, etc.).

2.6 Data we do NOT collect

  • ❌ We do not collect location data (GPS)
  • ❌ We do not collect usage data for analytics
  • ❌ We do not access contacts, photos, microphone or camera
  • ❌ We do not display third-party ads
  • ❌ We do not use advertising identifiers (AAID)
  • ❌ We do not sell your data

3. How we use the data

The data you provide is used solely to:

  • Calculate costs and profit margins for your printed parts
  • Generate sales and expense reports
  • Allow backup and restore of your own data
  • Unlock the Pro plan features after your purchase
  • Personalize the interface according to your preferences

4. Sharing with third parties

We do not share your data with third parties, except when strictly necessary for the features you chose to use:

  • Google LLC — provider of authentication (Sign-In), storage (Drive) and payment processing (Google Play Billing). See the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Storage and retention

  • Local data remains on your device while the app is installed. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.
  • Google Drive backups remain in your account until you decide to delete them.
  • JSON backup files exported to the Downloads folder remain there until you delete them.
  • Purchase history is kept by Google Play according to its platform policy; the app only stores a local validation token.

6. Your rights (LGPD / GDPR)

You have the right, at any time, to:

  • Access your data — all of it is visible inside the app itself
  • Export your data — using the "Export Backup" button in Settings
  • Correct or delete your data — directly inside the app, or via "Reset All"
  • Disconnect your Google account at any time, in Settings → Google Drive Sync
  • Revoke the app's access to your Google account at myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through the Google Play app

7. Security

Local data is stored in the app's private storage area, which is only accessible to Calc 3D on Android. Communication with Google Drive uses OAuth 2.0 and HTTPS. We do not store passwords; authentication is handled entirely by Google. Payments are processed by Google Play, and no financial data passes through the app.

8. Children

Calc 3D is intended for entrepreneurs and professionals aged 18 or older. The app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Android permissions used

  • INTERNET — required for Google Sign-In and Google Drive
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to detect when you are offline and disable cloud features
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (only on Android 9 or lower) — to save backups to the Downloads folder
  • com.android.vending.BILLING — required for the Google Play purchase system (Pro Lifetime and Monthly)

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be changed whenever there are significant changes.
